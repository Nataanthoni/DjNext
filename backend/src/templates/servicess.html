      FAQ

      What are web development services?‌

      Under the website development services, we understand building product architecture, backend and frontend engineering, quality assurance, and design. Software consultancy may also be a vital part of the development services company provides, so before actually hiring a software development team it’s worth making sure it has relevant experience in everything mentioned.

      How does the web development process work?‌

      Every web development service provider may establish its own process, but it’s a good practice to work within a Scrum framework with 10-day iterations. After the requirements are analyzed, we conduct the discovery phase, and then start developing the product. When the app is launched, we support and maintain it, and help with scaling.

      What technologies do you work on for web development?‌

      We apply several technologies to build backend and frontend (like Python, Django, Angular, React Native, and others) and a wide range of third-party integrations to empower the product with maps, banking data, or other services.

      Here at Django Stars we apply our extensive knowledge in both tech and business domains to help our partners build products from scratch, go through digital transformation, and scale.
