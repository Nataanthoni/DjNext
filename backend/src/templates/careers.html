Most business users have inboxes that are filled with emails to do with various processes they work on - sales, hiring, fundraising, deals & support. Streak lets them be successful at those processes because its built right into where they spend most of their day - the Inbox.

Our values

and commitment to you

Work smart, live well

Being effective & proud of your work is important. So is having a fulfilling personal life.

Growth opportunities

We learn and grow by taking people out of their comfort zones by giving them responsibility and ownership.

Pass the airplane test

We’d love to be stuck sitting next to anyone of our teammates on an international flight.

High collaboration, low meeting culture

We solve problems together but we don’t think meetings are the most effective (especially recurring ones!).

Profitable and proud

We’re a profitable company and sustainably growing. We focus on building for the long term happiness of our users and employees.

Goldilocks stage

We combine the metrics (and financial stability) of a larger company with all of the benefits of a small team moving at a fast pace.

Safe space

There are no questions you can’t ask. Streak provides the safety net so you can stretch and fail.

Letter from the founders

If you’re reading this that means you’re wondering what we have to say and what we’re about. What we’re about is pretty simple: We want to build a great product with a great team.
‍
We think a great product is one that provides value for users by solving a real pain.
‍
We think a great team is one where you’re surrounded by people who care about their work and are also kind, friendly and supportive. We know there are a lot of great people out there who think the same. A bunch of them are already working here at Streak, it’s something we're really proud of as founders.

The best way to understand what we’re talking about is to meet us. While we can’t meet everyone in person hopefully this site will give you a better idea about who we are. And if you like what you see here then let us know and hopefully you’ll be able to come on board and help make our product and team even greater.
‍
Aleem and Omar
